However, unemployment, wage and inflation play key roles in modern economy, especially for policy-makers. Wage vs Inflation

Labor Market

Let’s extend our analysis from short run to medium run, and start from labor market:

  • Workers are typically paid a wage that exceeds their reservation wage, the wage that would make them indifferent between working or being unemployed.

  • Wages typically depend on labor market conditions. The lower the unemployment rate, the higher the wages.

  • Bargaining power:

  • How much bargaining power a worker has depends first on the nature of the job. Unionize Amazon Workers

  • How much bargaining power a worker has also depends on labor market condition.

    • When the unemployment rate is low, it is more difficult for firms to find qualified replacement workers. Labor Shortage

    • When the unemployment rate is high, finding another job is harder for workers.

  • Economists call the theories that link productivity and efficiency of workers to the wage as efficiency wage theories.

    • Firms - such as high-tech firms - that see employee morale and commitment as essential to the quality of their work will pay more than firms in sectors where workers’ activities are more routine. Glassdoor
